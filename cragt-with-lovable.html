Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know to get started

What makes your magnets special? +

We use premium vinyl materials with vivid color reproduction and super-strong magnetic backing. Unlike cheap alternatives, our magnets stay vibrant and stick securely for years. Plus, every order gets personal attention from our family team.

How does the ordering process work? +

It's super simple! Choose your size, place your order, and we'll email you with easy instructions to upload your photos. We'll send you a proof for approval before printing. Once approved, we create your magnets and ship them via USPS.

What if I'm not satisfied with my order? +

We stand behind our quality 100%. If you're not completely happy within 30 days, just reach out and we'll make it right. We want you to absolutely love your magnets!

How long does shipping take? +

We process orders within 3-5 business days, then ship via USPS. Most orders arrive within 5-7 business days total. We only ship within the United States currently.

Can I customize the sizes or shapes? +

We currently offer three square sizes: 2"×2", 2.5"×2.5", and 4"×4". If you have a special request for custom sizing, feel free to email us at Magnetique.collective@gmail.com and we'll see what we can do!
